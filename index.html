My Timeline

2015-2017

Drama Club - Bahrain DODEA School

Upon joining the drama club at this high school, my fate of becoming an actor was sealed and I was ready to act my heart out.

2017-2018

Commercials - Florida, USA

I left the Bahrain DODEA School around the start of my senior year in high school and moved back to Jacksonville, Florida. While finishing my senior year, I auditioned for many commercials and landed a role in 3 of those commercials. I even landed my own agent too!

2017-2018

Modeling - Florida, USA

In addition to doing commercials during my senior year in high school, I also did a few modeling shoots as well. Well, I say a few, but I really only did three - which you can find on my resume!

2018-2021

Student - New York Film Academy, LA

The true beginning of my professional career as an actor. Many say that a lot of the stuff learned in college is unrelated to their major. For me, EVERYTHING was important. Between writing original scripts, directing and producing our own plays, and acting workshops, my time at NYFA is something I will always cherish. Not even the COVID-19 pandemic could stop our progress to becoming actors!

YouTube

I record videos of me playing my favorite video games while also annoying and getting laughs out of various players. I try to upload at least once a week and I try to keep the content fresh and exciting by attempting a wide variety of voices, but sometimes I need to go back to the classics like THE mouse.

Twitch

On certain days, I live stream playing video games by myself or with the best friend any guy could ask for and just mess about for a couple hours. This is a nice way for me to stay connected with my followers and check-in with everyone.

TikTok

When I can't think of any good video ideas to put on YouTube, I turn them into short videos on TikTok. Sometimes I upload more on TikTok than on YouTube, since the videos on YouTube require more thought and work into them, whereas I can do 5 different videos in 30 minutes for TikTok.
